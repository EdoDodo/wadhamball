Wadham College Ball 2016

ABOUT

If I had a world of my own?
Everything would be nonsense.

Join us on the eve of 24th June, as Wadham College prepares to re-imagine the iconic tale of Alice in Wonderland. Upon falling into a world of neon spectacles and psychedelic installations, guests will bring the historic tale screaming into the 21st century.

As darkness falls and clocks chime tick tock the present will transform into a nonsensical luminous grinning utopia. Because as the Cheshire Cat declares, imagination is the only weapon in the war against reality.

ENTERTAINMENT

Wadham Wonderland will create an interactive, kaleidoscopic and multi-dimensional space. Featuring... A juxtaposition of acoustic, pop and electro-music destinations.

Spectacular firework displays throwing neon illuminations. White rabbits encouraging you to explore the conceptual art and experimental installations hidden around our gardens. Euphoric thrills of magical fairground rides and hilarious live comedy.

FOOD & DRINK

Eat me. Drink me.

Wonderland will play host to a deliciously surreal feast, filled with gastronomic delights and wildly tasty concoctions. A three course meal to lull you into a state of synaesthetic wonder- even the Mad Hatter couldn't conjure up...

As you venture deeper into the rabbit hole, you'll discover a mesmerising display of culinary wonders to welcome you. The buzz of street food, sugary treats and hidden bars will cater to your every whim.

We have also curated a selection of the finest mixology that will mesmerise, shock and leave you curiouser and curiouser...

CHARITY

Wadham Ball will donate any leftover money from the ball to charity and also allow for an optional extra donation at ticket purchase.
